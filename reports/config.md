# Agro Strategic Report – Configuration

## Objective
Provide structured, reliable and decision-oriented agricultural intelligence
covering short, medium and long-term horizons.

## Temporal Layers
The system operates on three temporal layers:

1. Current campaign (immediate impact)
2. Next campaign (6–18 months)
3. Medium term (2–5 years)

## Priority Principle
Quality over quantity.
Only information that can influence decisions is included.

## Core Topics
- Regulation & policy
- Water & irrigation
- Crop-specific analysis
- Plant health & inputs
- Markets & costs
- Strategic risks & opportunities

## Geographic Scope
- Spain
- European Union
- International (when relevant)

## Output Structure
- Daily email briefing (always sent)
- Monthly living report
- Historical archive (repository)

## Exclusion Rules
The following are excluded:
- Opinion without technical backing
- Repeated news
- Non-scalable local cases
- Media noise without documentation
