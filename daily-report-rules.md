# Daily Report Rules

This document defines what information is included in the daily
Agro Strategic Report email.

## Time Horizon Balance

The daily report always includes:

### 1. Immediate / Current Year (High Priority)
- Current campaign information
- Regulatory changes already in force
- Market movements affecting current decisions
- Weather or risk alerts
- Technical alerts (pests, diseases, inputs)

### 2. Shortâ€“Medium Term (Selective)
- Announced regulations not yet in force
- Strategic trends impacting next campaigns
- Structural market signals

### 3. Long-Term (Context Only)
- Structural policy direction
- Major paradigm shifts
- Only included if they affect strategic decisions

## Exclusion Rule
No content is included only because it is news.
All content must:
- Affect decisions
- Affect risk
- Affect strategy
